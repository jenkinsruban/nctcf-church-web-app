WE BUILD UP

Surrendering your life to Jesus is only the beginning. Now it's time to go deeper.

As Christians walking through this world, we are called to pursue holiness and obedience to God's Word, becoming more and more like Christ (2 Corinthians 3:18). Growing in relationship with Jesus takes intentional action! NC Tamil Christian Fellowship offers a variety of ways to deepen our faith and become disciple-making disciples.

What's your next step?

Want to Grow in Christ?

Serve the body of Christ by joining a serve team or find a Small Group. There you will find community and lasting friendships.

Let's Pray Together

We are a Christ-abiding community. We believe in the power of prayer and make daily efforts to activate the very power of prayer in our lives. Prayer serves as both an offensive and defensive tool in our dealings with the physical and spiritual realms. Here, you can serve as a prayer warrior on behalf of other church family members, our pastoral staff and core leadership teams, local outreach ministries, global missionaries, and our world's many nations.
