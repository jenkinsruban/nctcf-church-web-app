About Our Fellowship

God is using United Body Of Christ to reach people with the Love of Christ and impact change in our community. United Body Of Christ is a place where you can grow in the knowledge of Christ, experience inspiring worship, and enjoy genuine fellowship. We welcome you to come and worship with us. We have a wonderful Sunday School program and a vibrant youth fellowship. We look forward for the opportunity to minister to you.

Who We Are?

The United Body Of Christ is a warm, welcoming, and friendly community, firmly rooted in the Word of God. We are a family church at heart with individuals in mind. UBOC is committed to proclaiming the gospel of Jesus Christ. We offer a place to connect with other believers in fellowship and to grow deeper in knowledge of the Bible, mature Spiritually, and become equipped to disciple.

Our Mission

Love God:

We commit to help and support the body of Christ be plugged in under the headship of Jesus Christ and receive the life giving resurrection power of God through the Holy Spirit

John 14:15,21,23,24 - 21
Galatians 5:22-23

Love One Another:

We commit to serve & enable brothers and sisters in Jesus Christ to be the ambassadors, sons and daughters, kings and queens, prophets, miracle workers, encouragers, cheerful givers, healers and teachers of God so as to live out the gospel of peace that Jesus has entrusted us

Matthew 28:18 - 20

Our Vision

Awake, Unite, Revive, Resurrect in Jesus's Name through the resurrection power of God by the indwelling & overflowing presence of the Holy Spirit

Awake!

be "actively waiting"

Unite!

be one body under the Lordship of Jesus Christ

Revive! (Resurrect!)
